🥚 Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Overview

  • Preparation time

    2 minutes

  • Cooking time

    8 minutes

  • Total time

    10 minutes

Scrambled eggs is one of the easiest breakfast dishes to dish, and they can make for a very healthy meal that is rich with protein and other important nutrients. I personally consider the following recipe the best way to make the tastiest scrambled eggs that you will ever come across.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ⅓ cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1 slice of butter

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add the milk, salt, and ground black pepper to the bowl.
  3. Whisk the ingredients together until well combined.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and melt the butter.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet.
  6. Using a spatula, continuously stir the eggs until they are cooked to your desired consistency.
  7. Remove the scrambled eggs from the skillet and serve hot with bread or toast.
  8. Bon appétit!
