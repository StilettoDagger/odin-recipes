üçõ Chicken Curry Recipe

Overview

  • Preparation time

    15 minutes

  • Cooking time

    1 hour

  • Total time

    1 hour 15 minutes

Chicken curry is and indian cuisine dish and possibly one of my all time favorite recipes to cook. It is incredibly flavorful and delicious and can make for a very nutritious lunch. The recipe that I personally use is somewhat non traditional but I can still guarantee that you will enjoy it.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken fillet
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 1 ginger
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of cooking cream
  • 2 cups of rice
  • 4 cups of water

Instructions

First, we need to prepare the curry sauce. After that, we can proceed to cook the rice and then serve the final dish with the rice and sauce mixed together.

First step: Making the curry sauce

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat and add some olive oil.
  2. Add the chopped onion, ginger, and garlic to the pan and saut√© until they become fragrant.
  3. Add the curry powder and stir well to coat the onions, ginger, and garlic.
  4. Add the chopped tomato and cook until it becomes soft.
  5. Pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a simmer.
  6. Let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  7. Pour the mixture into a blender and blend the mixture thoroughly until it becomes more fluid.
  8. Pour the mixture into a pot and heat it over medium heat
  9. Chop the chicken fillet into small dices and add it to the pot once the mixture gets hot enough.
  10. Leave the mixture to simmer for 15 minutes with the lid closed.
  11. Stir in the cooking cream and season with salt and ground black pepper.
  12. Simmer for another 5 minutes to thicken the sauce.

Second step: Making the rice

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water to remove excess starch.
  2. In a pot, add two cups of rinsed rice and 4 cups of water.
  3. Bring the water to a boil over high heat.
  4. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a lid.
  5. Simmer the rice for about 15-20 minutes or until all the water is absorbed.
  6. Remove the pot from heat and let it sit, covered, for 5 minutes.
  7. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.

Final step: Serving the final dish and adding some final touches

  1. Place a serving of rice on a plate.
  2. Add a generous portion of the chicken curry on top of the rice.
  3. Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley.
  4. Squeeze some lemon juice over the dish for added freshness.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
