Ingredients

  • 1 pound Spaghetti noodles
  • 1 pound minced beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Instructions

The recipe is split into two parts. In the first part you need to prepare the spaghetti sauce. The second part is where you will prepare the spaghetti noodles.

First step: Making the spaghetti sauce

  1. Peel the onions, then chop them into small dices. (Note: watch this video if you're unsure how to do it properly)
  2. Chop the tomatoes into small dices similar to how you chopped the onions.
  3. Peel the garlic, then mince them thoroughly using either a chopping knife or a mortar and pestle.
  4. Bring a sauce pan, add some olive oil and turn up the heat to medium-high.
  5. Add chopped onions to the heated pan and saute them for 5-10 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Once the onions have been sauted, add minced beef to the pan and let the beef cook for around 10 minutes until it turns brown.
  7. Add chopped tomatoes, tomato sauce, and season with salt and pepper. Then turn the heat down to low and let the mixture simmer for around 15 minutes.
  8. Optional: During the final minutes of cooking add a small cup of water to the mixture to thicken the sauce.

Second step: Cooking the spaghetti noodles

  1. Grab a pot and fill around half of it with water, then add a pinch of salt to the water filled pot.
  2. Place the pot on a stove and turn the heat to high for around 10 minutes to let the water mixture boil.
  3. Once the water mixture is finally boiling, turn down the heat to medium-low, add the spaghetti noodles and let them cook for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Carefully observe the noodles as they are being cooked, and turn off the heat once they become soft.
  5. After the noodles are done, turn off the heat and proceed to drain the water from the pot.
  6. Bring the pot back to the stove.

Final step: Mixing everything together and adding some final touches

  1. Pour the sauce from the sauce pan into the noodles pot.
  2. Reheat the pot under medium-low heat and leave to simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile add more seasoning to the pot when needed.
  4. Once that's done, turn off the heat and proceed to serve a plate of spaghetti üçù
  5. Optionally add toppings such as chopped parsley to the plate.
