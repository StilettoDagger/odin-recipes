Overview

  • Preparation time

    15 minutes

  • Cooking time

    20 minutes

  • Total time

Mashed potatoes is a simple yet delicious dish that can be both suitable as a side and main dish. It is very important that you make the potatoes creamy by adding a mixture of warm milk and butter as that can significantly enhance the texture of mashed potatoes. The following recipe used is optimal for making quick mashed potatoes, but you may also add some other additional ingredients as you please.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of potatoes
  • ½ cup of milk
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • ¼ cup of sour cream
  • Salt and pepper for seasoning

Instructions

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into small chunks.
  2. Place the potatoes in a large pot and cover them with water.
  3. Bring the water to a boil and cook the potatoes until they are tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  4. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot.
  5. Add the milk, butter, and sour cream to the pot.
  6. Mash the potatoes using a potato masher or a fork until smooth and creamy.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
