🥔 Mashed Potatoes Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of potatoes
  • ½ cup of milk
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • ¼ cup of sour cream
  • Salt and pepper for seasoning

Instructions

  1. Peel and cut the potatoes into small chunks.
  2. Place the potatoes in a large pot and cover them with water.
  3. Bring the water to a boil and cook the potatoes until they are tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  4. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot.
  5. Add the milk, butter, and sour cream to the pot.
  6. Mash the potatoes using a potato masher or a fork until smooth and creamy.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot and...
  9. Bon appétit!
